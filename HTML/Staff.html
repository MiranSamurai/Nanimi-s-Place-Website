Chef Nanami
Chef Nanami is a passionate Itamae, whose culinary journey began at the young age of 7. By 12, he had already mastered the art of sushi-making. With unmatched precision and skill, Chef Nanami sources the freshest ingredients, ensuring that every roll he crafts is a masterpiece. There isn’t a fish in the sea he hasn’t caught, his deep knowledge of seafood reflected in each delicate, flavorful creation. His dedication to the art of sushi-making and respect for traditional techniques set him apart, creating an unforgettable dining experience with each bite.
Dave The Diver
Our diver is skilled and passionate, always knowing exactly what he's doing beneath the waves. From anchovies to larger catches (though sharks might be a bit much!), he’s the key to our sushi’s exceptional quality. His expertise in sourcing the freshest seafood ensures every dish we serve is top-tier.
